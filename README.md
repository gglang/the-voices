Instructions for game:

1) Win by completing objectives and increasing your notoriety
2) Click on objectives for more details
3) Don't get caught by the police
4) Go to bed before the end of the day or get caught and exposed
4.1) WASD to move

You can do all sorts of stuff in this zaney wacky little world:
* kill people
* get caught by the police (not good)
* kill police (its harder)
* do stuff to corpses
* cook body parts
* eat cooked and uncooked food
* go insane (if you don't listen to the voices)
* give food to friends

BE CAREFUL! If you do illegal shit near people they will call the police!

P.S.: If there is a warrant out for your arrest, you can skin a corpse and wear the skin to clear your name.
